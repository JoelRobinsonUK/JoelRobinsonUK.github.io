VRCade

Abstract


The VRcade is a prototype VR arcade hub developed as a part of my dissertation exploring the question "How can virtual reality technology be used to revive the arcade experience for a contemporary audience?"
My aim was to design a diagetic game library that could serve as the access point for the games available in a brick and mortar VR arcade, and also work as a virtual social space. I concluded my report with the findings that while virtual reality would be a hugely beneficial partner to the ailing arcade industry, the opposite is also true: arcades could be the key to VR gaming becoming a mainstream platform.
