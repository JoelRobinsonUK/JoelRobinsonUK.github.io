Troubleâ€™s Brewing is a game about preparing potions to fulfil orders as quickly as possible. In the game the player must experiment to discover the effects of ingredients at their disposal and practice their technique at chopping, stirring, and boiling to produce the highest quality potions. Throughout the game the player will have the opportunity to upgrade their equipment and explore the story of the kingdom they work in.

The original iteration of Trouble's Brewing was built for VR, and I worked as part of a team with two other Bath Spa students. We presented a working prototype at the Bristol VR Lab in June 2019, and although we received positive feedback I felt that there was so much room to improve this project.

I modelled almost all of the assets in the game myself, and was directly responsible for a significant portion of the code. Both me and my teammate George designed the mechanics of the game.

Since that first iteration Trouble's Brewing has been the project I have most wanted to return to, so during a module focused on Digital Storytelling came up on my Master's course I took the opportunity. At time of writing I've completely restarted the project, only keeping visual assets from the original.

As a consequence of studying remotely I have not had access to VR equipment while redeveloping Trouble's Brewing, meaning I have had to reimagine it as a standard gameplay experience. If I had the chance I would willingly redo this project a third time to finally get it fully realised in Virtual Reality, as I belive it would really make good use of technology and the gameplay experience would be enhanced.
